Our Services

Air Conditioning Maintinance and Repair

Klondike’s comprehensive residential air conditioning service and repair evaluation will give you an affordable solution that can save you money on your heating/cooling energy bill, prevent repairs and replacements in the future, and extend the life of your current residential air conditioning system.

Furnace Maintinance and Repair

A well-maintained heater or furnace can yield considerable savings on your monthly energy bills, and ensures that you are meeting your equipment’s warranty requirements. Our tune-ups include a system cleaning, carbon monoxide testing for furnaces, and a complete 18-point inspection.

Gold Club Membership

Like anything of value that you’ve invested in, a properly functioning HVAC system needs a bi-annual tune-up and safety check. Klondike has a pre-season Gold Club Service Agreement that will help keep your equipment running safely and smoothly for years of comfort. Priority service during heat waves and no-worry scheduling are just a couple reasons our clients prefer the benefits of Gold Club.

Air Conditioning Installation

Free estimates include one of Klondike Air Conditioning Installation Service Orange County CA knowledgeable Comfort Advisors making a detailed assessment of your heating and cooling needs and providing you with an excellently designed plan and Good, Better, Best investment summaries to get you the system of your dreams. With same as cash financing available, there is no need to suffer another day when the weather turns extreme. All new installations include a complimentary year of Gold Club membership.
